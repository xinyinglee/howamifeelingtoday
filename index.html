EduLearn Platform

IS480: IS Application Project
 Team VI | Frontend Developer, Deputy Technical Lead

eduLearn Platform | IS480: IS Application Project

EduLearn is a web-based student monitoring & business analysis tool catered to enrichment centres in Singapore. Some of our motivations include enhancing the parent's involvement in their children's education, leveraging on QR codes to take attendance and schedule optimisation for operational purposes.

As the front-end developer & deputy technical lead, I assisted the team by coming up with pleasant UI designs, ensured design consistency, standards & usability of the system. The other developers and I also brainstormed on the schedule optimisation a;gorithm together as well.
