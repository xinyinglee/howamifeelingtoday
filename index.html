EzScheduling

IS360: Interactive Prototype & Design
 Team IV | Frontend Developer

EzScheduling

In the part time industry, supervisors are finding themselves in a situation where there is a increasing shortage of manpower. The reasons are mostly attributed to the demographics of the part timers, who are usually students. When situations such as a replacement of shift arises, the part timers have to manually source for their replacements themselves which can either be done by word of mouth, text messages, phone calls or instant messaging, making the process extremely tedious.

EzScheduling streamlines the process of part timers having to find a replacement when they are unable to attend their jobs. I assisted the team by collating observations, developing pleasant yet functional UI designs & ensuring that the application is human-centric by doing A/B testing. The poster above talks about how we kick-started the project and seeing the prototype form from start to end.

High-fidelity Prototype Technologies: Bootstrap with HTML5 & CSS3, jQuery

Seamless Bancassurance Claims Application Prototype

IS419: Retail Banking Process & Solutions Casabanca | Frontend Developer

Seamless Bancassurance Claims Applications

Bancassurance can be profitable for banks and insurance companies, whereby banks are able to earn additional revenue by selling insurance products, while insurance companies are able to expand their customer bases without having to expand their sales force or pay commissions to insurance agents or brokers.

However, some pain points within the process includes a complex claim process that is tedious and confusing to current policyholders, inflexible payout management, inconvenience in submitting claim documents, request forms and the claimant’s inability to keep track of current request This can reduce customer satisfaction towards the partner bank and insurance companies, resulting in diminishing customer satisfaction.

The recommendation for partner banks & insurance companies aims to fill the gaps of these pain points by streamlining the claims process and integrating into partner bank’s application. This includes creating a module to allow bank customers and policyholders to easily submit their claim requests and organise their documents via a e-Checklist and tapping on OCR technology. It also allows them to track their current claim progress, and once approved it allows direct depositing of claim amounts into the partner banks.

High-fidelity Prototype Technologies: Ionic Framework, Monaca IDE Online Debugging Tool
